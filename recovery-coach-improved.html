Pre-Training Snack 5 mins

Banana Rice Cakes with Peanut Butter & Honey

Quick-digesting carbohydrates for immediate muscle glycogen fuel.

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole grain rice cakes
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 1 sliced banana & drizzle of honey
Post-Training Snack 5 mins

Tart Cherry & Whey Recovery Smoothie

Rapid protein synthesis paired with tart cherry antioxidants for soreness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop Whey / Plant protein
  • 1/2 cup tart cherry juice & almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries & chia seeds
Nutritious Daily Snack 3 mins

Greek Yogurt Parfait with Pumpkin Seeds

Slow-digesting casein protein, zinc, and magnesium for deep tissue repair.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries & honey
