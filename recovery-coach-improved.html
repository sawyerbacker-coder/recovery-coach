Athletic Recovery Nutrition

Targeted recipes for energy, muscle repair, and inflammation control

Pre-Training Snack 5 mins

Banana Rice Cakes with Peanut Butter & Honey

Quick-digesting carbs for immediate muscle glycogen before training.

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole grain rice cakes
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 1 sliced ripe banana
  • 1 tsp raw honey & dash of cinnamon
Post-Training Snack 5 mins

Tart Cherry & Whey Recovery Smoothie

High protein for muscle synthesis plus tart cherry for soreness reduction.

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop Whey / Plant Protein
  • 1/2 cup tart cherry juice
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries & almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
Nutritious Daily Snack 3 mins

Greek Yogurt Parfait with Pumpkin Seeds

Rich in casein protein, zinc, and magnesium for muscle recovery.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp roasted pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries
  • 1 drizzle maple syrup
Nutritious Dinner 20 mins

Salmon Power Bowl with Quinoa & Asparagus

Omega-3 anti-inflammatory fats paired with complex recovery carbohydrates.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz grilled or baked salmon fillet
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup roasted asparagus & sweet potato
  • Olive oil, lemon & sea salt
